5.1.1
The OBD II system shall monitor all exhaust gas sensors (e.g., oxygen, air-fuel ratio, NOx) used for emission control system feedback (e.g., EGR control/feedback, SCR control/feedback, NOx adsorber control/feedback) or as a monitoring device for proper output signal, activity, response rate, and any other parameter that can affect emissions.
5.1.2
For vehicles equipped with heated exhaust gas sensors, the OBD II system shall monitor the heater for proper performance.
