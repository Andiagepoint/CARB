6.3.1
Except as provided in section (e)(6.3.2), the fuel system shall be monitored continuously for the presence of a malfunction.
6.3.2
Manufacturers shall define monitoring conditions for malfunctions identified in section (e)(6.2.1)(C) (i.e., air-fuel ratio cylinder imbalance malfunctions) in accordance with sections (d)(3.1) and (d)(3.2) (i.e., minimum ratio requirements).
